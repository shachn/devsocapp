stuff i've done :3

pc builds

Started flipping pcs in 2023-2024 - my boyfriend and I would buy secondhand parts on fb marketplace, build pcs & sell them. Learnt a lot about hardware and ended up building my own pc as well. He did a lot of the building & cable management and I was responsible for setting up software, benchmarking, and troubleshooting any issues.

3d printing + laser engraving

My dad has a Bambu Lab A1 printer. I've borrowed it a lot over the years to print various things - pc cases, storage containers, display holders, and keycaps. I also have a creality falcon 10w laser engraver, it works well with wood, acrylic & aluminium. I'm currently laser cutting the front cover for a NAS.

doing nails

I started doing my own nails in 2023, moved on to making press-on nails soon after, and started posting on instagram. I grew a pretty decent following and went to a couple artist market events in sydney and melbourne, which was really fun. Currently, I'm not doing press-ons anymore and am doing nails on people now :) Check out my instagram in the links section below.
